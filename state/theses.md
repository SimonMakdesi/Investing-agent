## 4. Lessons learned / open questions

- **Forced-seller vs fundamental-seller distinction matters:** ATRLJ entry was gated on identifying Varma's exit as technical (block liquidation at discount) rather than fundamental — this is the right analytical step before acting on any drawdown + insider combo. Always ask "why is the other side selling?"
- **Industrivärden as signal amplifier:** When Industrivärden participates (Essity, Volvo), it is categorically higher-information than a single management buy — strategic, long-duration, non-tax-driven holder with full board visibility. Weight distinctly.
- **Screener bug — net insider flow (unresolved):** Screener still summing gross Förvärv while ignoring same-day Avyttring. Confirmed false positives on SOBI, AAK, BILL. Manual cross-check required on every new cluster signal until fixed.
- **Open question:** Volvo, Essity, and Thule all report within a ~7-day window (Jul 23–29). Current sizing (4.8%, 7.7%, 3.6%) is conservative but concentrated in time. Consider whether any light trimming ahead of that window is warranted, or whether conviction holds at current sizes.
