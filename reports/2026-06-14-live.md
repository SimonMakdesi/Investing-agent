- Price is still falling hard (-25% in 1m and 3m alike) despite the insider buys — the market is pricing in something the buying hasn't fixed yet. Catching a falling knife on a leveraged balance sheet is the textbook way to lose money.

**Insider read.** Strongest possible insider signal on Large Cap: the controlling owner doubling down personally with material sums (~110 MSEK) in two waves, the second after the stock fell another 25%. Per constitution, insider buying is a strong signal — and here it's the clearest variant of it.

**Price read.** Down 51% in 12m, 25% in the last month alone, 31% below the 200-day MA, vol 62%. This is a stock in capitulation. Insider buying into capitulation is the setup we look for in the Aggressive sleeve — but capitulation can continue, and Q2 earnings on ~Jul 22 (~38 days out) is a binary event that could resolve the question either way.
